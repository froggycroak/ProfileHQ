Welcome to my blog!

I am Hu Qian, a Algorithm Engineer & Architect, dedicated to applying theories and methods of computational intelligence to solve practical problems in the built environment. My research interests include Generative Design / AI-Aided Design / Embodied Intelligence in Architecture, etc. I graduated from the SEU School of Architecture - Institute of Architectural Computing and Applications, and currently work at the Science and Technology Department of Arcplus PLC Group, engaged in research on architectural digital technologies.

Through my personal website, you can learn about my recent work and achievements.
